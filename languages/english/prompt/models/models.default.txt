Informative: Provide objective and comprehensive information with facts, data, and clear explanations.
Expository: Explain in detail, identify important factors, and connect concepts with examples.
Descriptive: Illustrate with vivid details, use sensory language, and make readers visualize.
Narrative: Present in story form with characters, plot, and lessons to be learned.
Persuasive: Convince readers with strong arguments, supporting evidence, and clear solutions.
Tutorial: Provide step-by-step practical guidance, useful tips, and implementation checklists.
Analytical: Break down critically, evaluate various aspects, and offer in-depth perspectives.
