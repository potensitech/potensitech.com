## Tips for Maximizing Battery Life

Even with the best battery technology, proper usage can significantly extend the lifespan of a smartphone. Here are some tips for The Potensians to get the most out of their affordable Apple smartphone:

### Adjust Settings

- **Lower Screen Brightness**: Reducing brightness can save a significant amount of energy.
- **Enable Low Power Mode__: This feature reduces background activity and optimizes performance for longer battery life.
- **Turn Off Unused Features__: Disabling Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, or location services when not in use can conserve power.

### Regular Maintenance

- **Update iOS Regularly**: Software updates often include battery optimization features.
- **Avoid Extreme Temperatures**: High or low temperatures can degrade battery performance over time.
- **Use Certified Accessories**: Non-certified chargers and cables can harm the battery.
