### Key Takeaways from the Comparison

- **M1 Chip**: Introduced in 2020, the M1 chip marked Apple's departure from Intel processors. It offers impressive speed and energy efficiency, making it an excellent choice for students and professionals on a budget.

- **M2 Chip**: Launched in 2022, the M2 chip builds on the success of the M1. It provides enhanced graphics capabilities and better overall performance, making it ideal for creative professionals and multitaskers.

- **Intel Core i5**: While still capable of handling basic tasks, the Intel Core i5 struggles with power efficiency and performance compared to Apple's custom silicon. It is best suited for those who prioritize affordability over cutting-edge technology.
