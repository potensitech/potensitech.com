### Smart HDR 5: Elevating Dynamic Range

High Dynamic Range (HDR) technology has reached new heights with Smart HDR 5 on these devices. By analyzing each frame pixel-by-pixel before merging them into one cohesive image file format (HEIF), colors appear more vibrant than ever before—whether it's autumn leaves scattered along park pathways or burgers sizzling on grills during summer barbecues.

### ProRAW & ProRes Video Recording

For serious photographers among The Potensians who demand greater control over their work, post-capture editing becomes effortless thanks to access directly within native apps and support for ProRAW and ProRes formats. These features allow for professional-grade editing, giving photographers the freedom to adjust exposure, color balance, and other elements with precision.

ProRAW combines the benefits of RAW image capture with Apple’s computational photography, offering a perfect blend of detail and flexibility. Meanwhile, ProRes video recording ensures high-quality footage, making it ideal for content creators who want to produce cinematic videos.
