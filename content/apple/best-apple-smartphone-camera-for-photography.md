The iPhone 15 Pro Max stands tall as the flagship model for photography enthusiasts. For those who prefer a slightly smaller device without compromising on camera quality, the iPhone 15 Pro is an excellent choice. The iPhone 15 provides a more budget-friendly option while still delivering high-quality photography. For The Potensians who prefer a compact device, the iPhone SE (2026) offers a single-lens camera that punches above its weight. While it lacks some of the advanced features of the Pro models, it still delivers excellent image quality, making it a great choice for those on a budget.
