## Optical vs Digital Image Stabilization

### Understanding the Basics

Before diving into Xiaomi’s offerings, it’s essential to understand the two primary types of image stabilization technologies available in smartphones: **optical image stabilization (OIS)**and**digital image stabilization (DIS)**.

- **Optical Image Stabilization (OIS):** This technology physically adjusts the lens or sensor to counteract movements. It’s particularly effective in low-light conditions or when capturing high-motion scenarios, such as sports or wildlife photography. 
- **Digital Image Stabilization (DIS):** DIS relies on software algorithms to stabilize footage. While it’s useful, it may not perform as well as OIS in challenging lighting conditions or during rapid movements.
