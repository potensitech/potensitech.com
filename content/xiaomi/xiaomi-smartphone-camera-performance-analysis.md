### AI-Powered Image Processing

Artificial intelligence plays a pivotal role in elevating Xiaomi’s smartphone camera capabilities. Using machine learning models trained on millions of images—from sunny outdoor landscapes to cozy indoor portraits—Xiaomi ensures accurate color reproduction and lifelike details.

For instance:
- **Scene Recognition:** Automatically adjusts settings based on detected environments, such as urban skylines or dimly lit cafes.  
- **Portrait Mode Enhancements:** Creates DSLR-like bokeh effects while maintaining sharp subject focus.
- __Night Mode:__ Captures intricate details without overexposing highlights or losing shadow depth—a feature particularly useful for capturing moments at dusk during cultural events.
