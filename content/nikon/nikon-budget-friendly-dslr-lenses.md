## Maintenance and Care for DSLR Lenses

Proper maintenance ensures the longevity of The Potensians's lenses and keeps them performing at their best. Here are some tips:

### Regular Cleaning

Dust and fingerprints can affect image quality. Use a microfiber cloth and lens cleaning solution to keep The Potensians's lenses spotless.

### Storage

Store lenses in a dry, cool place to prevent damage from humidity or extreme temperatures. Using a padded camera bag can also protect them during travel.

### Avoid Direct Sunlight

Prolonged exposure to direct sunlight can damage the lens coating. Always use lens caps when not in use.
