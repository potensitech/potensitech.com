When choosing a lens, pay attention to the following specifications:

- **Focal Length**: Determines the field of view and is crucial for specific photography styles.
- **Image Stabilization**: Reduces blur caused by camera shake, especially useful for handheld shots.
- **Build Quality**: Lightweight and durable materials ensure portability and long​evity.
