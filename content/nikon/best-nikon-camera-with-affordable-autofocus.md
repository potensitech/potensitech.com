## Superior Autofocus Technology

Autofocus is a critical feature for any camera, and Nikon excels in this area. With advanced autofocus systems, Nikon cameras ensure sharp, precise images in any situation. Let’s explore the technology behind Nikon’s autofocus systems.

### Phase-Detection Autofocus

Phase-detection autofocus is ideal for capturing fast-moving subjects. This technology uses dedicated sensors to measure the distance between the lens and the subject, allowing for quick and accurate focus. It’s perfect for sports photography, wildlife, or any scenario where speed is essential.

### Contrast-Detection Autofocus

Contrast-detection autofocus is best suited for still photography. By analyzing the contrast in an image, this system ensures that the subject is in perfect focus. It’s ideal for portraits, food photography, or capturing intricate details.

### Hybrid Autofocus Systems

Many Nikon cameras combine phase-detection and contrast-detection autofocus into a hybrid system. This ensures seamless performance across different shooting scenarios, making these cameras versatile and reliable.
