## Lenses Every Beginner Should Consider

A camera body is only part of the equation; lenses play an equally vital role in achieving breathtaking shots. Here are three beginner-friendly [lens options](/nikon/budget-friendly-nikon-dslr-lens):

### 1. **AF-P DX NIKKOR 18-55mm f/3.5-5.6G VR**
- This versatile zoom lens is often bundled with entry-level cameras like the D3500. 
- It’s perfect for everyday use—whether photographing landscapes in Central Park or portraits during festive occasions. 
- Its Vibration Reduction (VR) feature minimizes blurriness caused by shaky hands—a blessing in disguise! 

### 2. **AF-S DX NIKKOR 35mm f/1.8G**
- A prime lens known for its sharpness and low-light performance. 
- Ideal for street photography or indoor shots with limited lighting. 
- Its compact size makes it easy to carry around, whether exploring a local farmer’s market or capturing the vibrant nightlife of Times Square. 

### 3. **AF-S NIKKOR 50mm f/1.8G**
- Often referred to as the "nifty fifty," this lens is a favorite among beginners and professionals alike. 
- It delivers stunning bokeh (background blur), making it perfect for portraits. 
- The lens is versatile enough to handle a variety of scenarios, from close-ups of flowers to detailed food photography.
