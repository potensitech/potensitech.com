## Tips for Choosing the Right Lens​

When selecting a budget-friendly Nikon DSLR lens, consider the following factors:

### 1. Purpose What type of photography do The Poten​sians enjoy most? If landscapes are the focus, an ultra-wide-angle lens is ideal. For portraits, a prime lens like the 50mm f/1.8G is a better choice.

### 2. Compatibility Ensure the lens is compatible with The Potensians' camera model. Nikon offers lenses for both DX (crop sensor) and FX (full-frame) cameras, so double-check before purchasing.

### 3. Budget Set a budget and stick to it. While it’s tempting to splurge on high-end lenses, there are plenty of affordable options that deliver excellent performance.

### 4. Weight and Portability If The Potensians plan to travel frequently, a lightweight lens is essential. Carrying heavy equipment can be cumbersome, especially during long shoots.
