## Key Models for 2025

As of 2025, Nikon offers several entry-level DSLR models that cater to hobbyists. Let’s take a closer look at some of the most popular options:

### Nikon D3500

The Nikon D3500 remains a favorite among beginners due to its affordability and ease of use. Its 24.2-megapixel sensor ensures high-quality images, while the "Guide Mode" sim​plifies the learning process for new photographers. The camera also boasts an impressive battery life, capable of capturing up to 1,550 shots on a single charge.

### Nikon D5600

For hobbyists seeking more advanced features, the Nikon D5600 is an excellent choice. It includes a vari-angle touchscreen, making it easier to shoot from creative angles. Additionally, its SnapBridge technology allows seamless transfer of photos to smartphones, enabling instant sharing on social media.

### Nikon D7500

While slightly more advanced, the Nikon D7500 is still accessible to hobbyists looking to push their skills further. It offers 4K video recording, a 20.9-megapixel sensor, and a weather-sealed body—perfect for outdoor photography enthusiasts.
