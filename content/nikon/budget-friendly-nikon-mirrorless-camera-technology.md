## Why Choose Mirrorless Cameras?

Mirrorless cameras have become increasingly popular due to their compact design and versatility. Unlike traditional DSLR cameras that rely on mirrors to reflect light into an optical viewfinder, mirrorless models eliminate this mechanism entirely. This results in lighter bodies and faster performance—ideal for capturing moments during events like Independence Day fireworks or Memorial Day parades.
