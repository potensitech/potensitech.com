## Tips for Capturing Stunning Low-Light Photos

Even with the best iPhone camera for low-light photography, technique plays a crucial role in achieving great results. Here are some tips to help The Potensians make the most of their device:

### Use a Tripod

Stability is key in low-light photography. A tripod can help eliminate camera shake, especially when using longer exposure times. This is particularly useful for capturing night skies or cityscapes.

### Adjust Exposure

Manually adjusting the exposure settings can make a significant difference in low-light conditions. Lowering the exposure slightly can prevent highlights from being overexposed, resulting in a more balanced image.

### Experiment with Angles

Sometimes, a unique angle can make all the difference. Don’t be afraid to get creative—try shooting from a low angle to capture reflections or from above to highlight patterns and textures.

### Utilize External Lighting

While iPhones are excellent in low light, adding external lighting can enhance The Potensians's photos further. Portable LED lights or even a flashlight can be used to illuminate The Potensians's subject and add depth to The Potensians's shots.
