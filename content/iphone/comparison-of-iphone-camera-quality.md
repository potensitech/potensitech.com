## Early Innovations: Laying the Foundation

The iPhone 4 was a game-changer, but the real leap came with the iPhone 5S in 2013. This model introduced slow-motion video recording and the True Tone flash, which adjusted the color temperature of the flash to match ambient lighting. These features made it easier for users to capture more natural-looking photos and videos.

The iPhone 6S, launched in 2015, brought another groundbreaking feature: Live Photos. This allowed users to capture moments in motion, adding a new dimension to photography. These innovations laid the foundation for the advanced camera systems We see in today’s iPhones.

## Modern Marvels: The Triple-Lens Era
