## Tips When Shopping for Cheap iPhones

### Check Network Compatibility

Before purchasing, confirm that the iPhone model is compatible with The Potensians' carrier and region. This ensures smooth connectivity for calls, browsing, and streaming.

### Consider Storage Needs

Storage is a key factor when choosing an iPhone. Here’s a quick guide:

- **64GB:** Best for casual users who primarily use their phones for calls, messaging, and light app usage.
- **128GB or Higher:** Ideal for avid photographers, gamers, or anyone with a large app library.

### Compare Prices Across Platforms

Search for deals on trusted platforms like **Amazon Renewed**, **Apple Certified Refurbished**, and **Best Buy**. Always check seller ratings and reviews to ensure a safe and reliable purchase.

## Additional Considerations for 2026

### Battery Health and Longevity

When buying a refurbished or older iPhone, always check the battery health. Apple’s Battery Health feature provides insights into the device’s maximum capacity and performance. A battery with at least 80% capacity is generally considered good.

### Warranty and Return Policies

Opt for sellers that offer warranties or return policies. This adds an extra layer of security in case the device doesn’t meet expectations. Apple’s Certified Refurbished program is a reliable choice, offering a one-year warranty.

### The Role of 5G in 2026

With 5G networks becoming the standard in 2026, selecting a device with 5G capabilities ensures faster internet speeds and better connectivity. Models like the **iPhone SE (3rd Gen)** and **iPhone 12** are excellent choices for those looking to future-proof their purchase.
