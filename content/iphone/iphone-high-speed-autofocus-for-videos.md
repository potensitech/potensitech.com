## How Does High-Speed Autofocus Work?

To understand the marvel behind **iPhone high-speed autofocus for videos**, it’s essential to explore its underlying mechanics. At its core, autofocus systems rely on sensors that measure contrast or phase differences within a scene to determine focus points. Apple has elevated this concept by integrating advanced machine learning algorithms and state-of-the-art hardware components, such as LiDAR scanners (available in Pro models). These technologies enable the camera to lock onto subjects faster than ever before, even in challenging conditions like low light or fast-moving scenarios.

The LiDAR scanner, in particular, plays a pivotal role. By emitting laser pulses and measuring the time it takes for them to return, the scanner creates a detailed depth map of the scene. This allows the iPhone to focus on subjects with remarkable speed and accuracy, regardless of the environment.
