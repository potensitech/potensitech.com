Apple's Metal API plays a pivotal role in enhancing graphical efficiency across apps related to iPhones. Designed specifically for their ecosystem, Metal provides developers with low-level access to GPU resources while reducing overheads associated with traditional APIs like OpenGL ES or Vulkan.
