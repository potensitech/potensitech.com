## Key Components of a Smartphone Chipset

To better understand the significance of chipsets, let's break down their key components:

### CPU (Central Processing Unit)

The CPU is the core of the chipset, responsible for executing instructions and performing general computing tasks. It determines how quickly apps launch, how efficiently they run, and how well the phone handles multitasking.

### GPU (Graphics Processing Unit)

The GPU handles all visual elements, including gaming graphics, video playback, and rendering animations. A powerful GPU ensures a smooth and immersive visual experience, especially for gamers and video enthusiasts.

### AI Engine

The AI engine powers intelligent features such as voice assistants, facial recognition, and camera enhancements. It enables the phone to learn and adapt to The Potensians' usage patterns, making the device smarter over time. 

### Modem

The modem ensures seamless connectivity by supporting 4G LTE or 5G networks. In 2025, 5G compatibility is a critical factor for users who demand faster internet speeds and lower latency.

### ISP (Image Signal Processor)

The ISP enhances photo and video quality by optimizing colors, reducing noise, and improving dynamic range. It is a crucial component for photography enthusiasts who want to capture professional-quality images.
