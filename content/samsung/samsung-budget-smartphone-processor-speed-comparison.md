## Core Metrics to Evaluate Processor Speed

When comparing Samsung's budget smartphone processors, several technical metrics come into play:

### Clock Speed

Clock speed, measured in GHz (gigahertz), indicates how fast a processor can execute instructions per second. Higher clock speeds generally translate to faster performance, especially for single-threaded tasks like opening apps or browsing the web.

### Core Count

The number of cores in a processor determines its multitasking capabilities. More cores allow the processor to handle multiple tasks simultaneously, such as streaming a video while downloading files in the background. For The Potensians, who often switch between apps like Instagram and YouTube, this is a crucial factor.

### Architecture Modern processors are designed with energy-efficient architectures that balance performance and battery life. Samsung's budget smartphones often feature processors optimized for everyday tasks, ensuring that users get the most out of their devices without frequent charging.
