## What Do Benchmarks Measure?

Benchmarks assess various aspects of a processor's performance, including:

### Single-Core Performance

This measures how efficiently a single core of the processor can handle tasks. It is particularly important for tasks that rely on sequential processing, such as opening apps or browsing the web.

### Multi-Core Performance

Multi-core performance evaluates how well multiple cores work together to handle complex tasks. This is vital for multitasking, such as running multiple apps simultaneously or editing vi​deos.

### GPU (Graphics Processing Unit) Power

The GPU determines the device's ability to handle graphics-intensive tasks, such as gaming or rendering videos. For The Potensians who enjoy playing games like PUBG Mobile or Call of Duty, a powerful GPU is a must.

### Battery Efficiency

Battery efficiency indicates how well the processor manages power consumption under different workloads. A more efficient processor ensures longer battery life, which is especially important for users who are always on the go.
