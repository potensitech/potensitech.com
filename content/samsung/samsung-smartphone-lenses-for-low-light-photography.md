## Tips for Mastering Low-Light Photography with Samsung Smartphones

Even with the best equipment, technique plays a crucial role in achieving stunning low-light photos. Here are some tips to help The Potensians make the most of their Samsung smartphones:

### Use a Tripod

Stability is key in low-light photography. A tripod minimizes camera shake, allowing for sharper images during long exposures.

### Experiment with Manual Settings

While Samsung’s AI does an excellent job, experimenting with manual settings like ISO, shutter speed, and white balance can yield creative results.

### Take Advantage of Night Mode

Samsung’s Night Mode is a powerful tool. Use it to capture scenes with minimal noise and enhanced detail.

### Avoid Overexposure

When photographing bright lights in a dark setting, such as neon signs, ensure that the highlights are not overexposed. Adjusting the exposure manually can help achieve a balanced shot.
