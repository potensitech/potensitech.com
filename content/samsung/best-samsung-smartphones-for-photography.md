- **Flex Mode Shooting:** Allows hands-free photos by propping up the device at different angles—a great feature for group gatherings or solo adventures.

The Galaxy Z Fold 5 is not just a smartphone; it’s a tool for innovation. Its foldable design adds a layer of creativity, enabling The Potensians to experiment with new perspectives.
