### Key Components of a Smartphone Chipset

A typical smartphone chipset consists of several components working together seamlessly:

1. **CPU (Central Processing Unit):** Handles general computing tasks. 
2.  __GPU (Graphics Processing Unit):__ Powers gaming and graphic-intensive applications. 
3. **Modem:** Ensures seamless connectivity with cellular networks like 4G LTE or 5G. 
4. **ISP (Image Signal Processor):** Processes images captured by cameras for optimal quality. 
5. **AI Engine:** Enhances features like voice recognition and photo optimization. 

For example, consider Samsung's Exynos 2200 chipset launched in early 2025—this powerful processor integrates AMD RDNA 2 architecture for unparalleled graphics performance while supporting cutting-edge AI capabilities.
